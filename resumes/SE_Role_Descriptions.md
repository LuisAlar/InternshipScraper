# General Role Descriptions

The following descriptions focus on the overarching responsibilities, the scope of your role, and the value you bring to the organization, rather than listing specific metric-driven achievements.

---

### Orbital Mentorship — DevOps Lead
In my role as DevOps Lead, I am responsible for managing and optimizing the organization's cloud infrastructure and internal deployment pipelines. My primary focus is on standardizing development environments, reducing configuration friction for new team members, and ensuring the reliability of our backend services. I oversee the implementation of containerization strategies and build observability tools to monitor system performance, proactively identifying and resolving network bottlenecks to maintain long-term platform stability.

### Xtensible Solutions — Solutions Engineer Intern
As a Solutions Engineer Intern, my primary responsibility was bridging the gap between software development and IT operations by automating critical infrastructure workflows. I was tasked with designing and maintaining scripts that managed cloud resources, streamlined instance creation, and integrated with source control systems. A significant portion of my role involved developing automated API testing frameworks and collaborating with engineering leadership to continuously monitor system health, report defects, and ensure seamless delivery pipelines prior to production deployment.

### Indigo Magazine — Web Development Lead & Creative Director
Serving as both Web Development Officer and Creative Director, I lead the technical and visual direction of the publication's digital presence. My role involves overseeing cross-functional collaboration between developers, writers, and designers to ensure a cohesive user experience. I am responsible for architecting scalable web platforms, managing cloud-based asset delivery systems, and building internal content management tools that empower editorial staff. My work fundamentally bridges UI/UX design with robust backend administration to support high-traffic digital marketing campaigns.

### YourOwn — Team Lead & System Architect
As the Team Lead and System Architect, I direct the high-level technical strategy and oversee the full-stack development lifecycle for the platform. My responsibilities center on defining the system's core architecture, ensuring scalable data management, and guiding the transition from monolithic structures to modular, feature-based designs. I coordinate engineering efforts to implement advanced state synchronization, establish automated testing protocols, and maintain rigorous deployment standards, ultimately ensuring the platform delivers a consistent, performant, and reliable user experience.
